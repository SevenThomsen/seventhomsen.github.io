Contender Approaching

Tournament | Sole Organizer, Streamer, & Graphic Designer

Contender Approaching was the first Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Weekly hosted by Contender Esports. I designed the graphics, logo (incorporating their brand), and schedule. I have personally been the on-site TO every Monday since CA 1 (February 10th, 2020). This includes running the bracket, seeding, calling matches, and managing the stream during 40+ person events. I also help market the tournament to our local scene and keep them up to date on any changes. I created QR codes and stagelists sheets on laminated paper handouts to help attendees at the event. At the event, I use a custom game branded game mods that I've created, which can be seen below. Streamed live on TV_Seven. I also cut and upload the VODs from the tournament to Springfield Smash.

Ursa Major / Ursa Minor

Tournament | Organizer, Streamer, Graphic Designer, Bracket Manager, Marketer

Ursa is a tournament series hosted at Missouri State University after I became the Game Coordinator. As a successor tournament to Respect the Bair, it continues the MSU branding but introduces new improvements to the series. Ursa Major is a monthly tournament hosted on Saturday. Ursa Minor is a more frequent weekly tournament at a different building at MSU. Both feature an extremely advanced and custom HTML overlay, transitions, and more. Custom branded stages and menus are also included. This makes Ursa Major Springfield Missouri's premier tournament. Singles uses a waterfall bracket, and it also has doubles.

Quaranfiends

Tournament | Streamer, Graphic Designer, Bracket Manager

Quaranfiends was the successor to a tournament called 'Neutral B'. After Neutral B's cancellation from COVID-19 at the start of 2020, Quaranfiends would later open in the same venue while requireing proof of vaccination to ensure the safety of all players. Later, it would be only masks required. The tournament featured the amenities of Sunshine Lanes Bowling Alley, custom graphics, accepting card or cash at the door, and a high production value. VODs are uploaded on the Springfield Smash YouTube.
